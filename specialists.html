Our Specialists

Marta Garcia
Specialist in Cornrows and twist

Marta Garcia is a specialist in cornrows. She completed a three-year hairdressing school in Equatorial Guinea before moving to Canada. Marta has a passion for intricate braid patterns and has continued to perfect her skills through various workshops and training sessions. Her expertise lies not only in traditional cornrows but also in creating modern and trendy styles that appeal to a wide range of clients. Marta is known for her meticulous attention to detail and her ability to create long-lasting, beautiful cornrows.

Elrize Adele
Specialist in Ponytails and locs

Elrize Adele hails from Cameroon and has been trained in hairdressing for over 10 years. She has been practicing her craft in Canada for the past 5 years and is highly renowned for her expertise in ponytails. Elrize is unmatched in her ability to create sleek, stylish ponytails that stay in place all day. Her reputation is unparalleled, with clients often praising her for the flawless finish and creative designs she brings to each style. Elrize's dedication to her clients and her craft has made her a sought-after stylist with a loyal following.

Ingride Edwin
Specialist in Braids

Originating from the Democratic Republic of Congo, Ingride Edwin boasts 7 years of experience in hairdressing, particularly in creating stunning hairstyles and cuts. She excels in braids and has had the privilege of working with Canadian celebrities to craft their glamorous looks for stage performances. Ingride's artistic vision and technical skills make her a standout in the industry. Her ability to transform hair into works of art has earned her accolades and a prestigious reputation among her peers and clients alike.
